ROBERT BROWN STANDINGS 2025 - 2026 (JAN)

Instructions

1. The Robert Brown Cup is played over the course of a year and is awarded to the most consistent pair through that year. Points are awarded towards the Cup at sessions occurring on the last Thursday of each month from April to March.

2. Your score only qualifies towards the Cup if you are playing with your regular partner.

3. The scores for each session are calculated as follows:

a. From the ranking table for the session, the results for non-regular partners are removed.

b. The top pair of those remaining is awarded points equal to the number remaining pairs.

c. Each subsequent pair is awarded one point less. The final pair being awarded one point. If any of the places are tied, the pairs share the points for the respective places.

d. The seven best scores for each pair are added together to determine their final score.
